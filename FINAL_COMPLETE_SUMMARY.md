## üí° Key Learnings

### 1. SOPs vs Training Modules
**SOPs** = Process workflows with detailed steps
**Training Modules** = Educational content about products/concepts

### 2. Content Field Types
- **Rich Text**: Use string for `content` field, not array
- **JSON Arrays**: Store as stringified JSON: `"[\"item1\", \"item2\"]"`

### 3. Entry Titles
All content types have a `title` field to prevent "Untitled" entries in the UI.
